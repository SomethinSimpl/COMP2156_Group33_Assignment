George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
It has multiple campuses across Toronto and provides modern labs, experienced instructors, and industry relevant training.
In addition to the Casa Loma campus, George Brown also operates the St. James and Waterfront campuses.
Each campus specializes in different program areas such as technology, business, health sciences, hospitality, and design.
It offers a wide range of diploma, certificate, and postgraduate programs.
The college focuses on practical, hands on learning to help students prepare for real world careers.
